## Standard reset procedure

1. Verify the member out of band (callback to the number on file — never the
   number in the inbound request). Recognizing a voice or face is **not** an
   authentication factor; voice cloning and deepfakes defeat it (see the 2024
   Arup deepfake-CFO case, ~US$25.6M lost).
2. In Microsoft Entra ID, open the user, choose "Reset password," and require a
   passkey (FIDO2) re-enrollment. Passkeys are phishing-resistant; SMS OTP is not.
3. Confirm MFA method is a passkey, not push. MFA-fatigue (push bombing) defeats
   plain push approval.

## Troubleshooting

If the member reports "impossible travel" lockouts, check whether the login geo
matches their known location before unlocking. Do not unlock on the member's say-so.
