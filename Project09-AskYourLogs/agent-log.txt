SUMMARY OF INTERVENTIONS
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- The agent was useful for the FAST first pass (finding relevant 14:05 lines).
- The agent was insufficient for CORRELATION and for CAUSE-vs-SYMPTOM, which
  required trace_id joins and human reading of the fields.
- I never pasted an AI answer into the report without checking it against the
  raw log lines. Every filter above was audited before use.
- Net: AI saved time on retrieval; the judgment (root cause, affected-user
  list, "what to fix") stayed with me.
